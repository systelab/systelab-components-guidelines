Switch

Switches are the preferred way to adjust settings. They're used to control binary options: think On/Off or True/False.

Principles

Use switches to:

  • Toggle a single item on or off
  • Immediately activate or deactivate something

Anatomy

A switch is composed by a track and a thumb and a mandatory label text.

Label text

Switches should always be paired with an inline label describing the option that the switch controls, as well as its state.

Label shoud be paced in the left.

Placement

Switches are often arranged in stacked layouts.

Behaviour

A switch is successfully toggled when the switch thumb slides to the other side of the track upon user interaction.

When a user toggles a switch, its thumb size changes and the corresponding action takes effect immediately.
